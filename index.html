ABOUT CAMELOT

There's a lot more to Camelot than meets the eye.

The King

The King is the highest ranking officer

The High Council

The high council is made up of the Realm Advisors and the High Offices

Realm Advisors

Realm Advisors are members of the High Council and run the clan in absence of the King

Head Knight

An advisor to the High Council from the knights. Usually a former Realm Advisor whose advice is sought.

Master of Squires

Manager, advisor, recruiter and leader of the squires.

Master at Arms

Responsible to help the clan achieve a competitive edge in tournaments and competitive play. Is supported by Sergeant of Arms.

Emissary

Represents and promotes the clan in any way shape or form. Manages community issues and advises the clan on diplomatic matters.

The Knight's Assembly

The Knight's Assembly is empowered with many clan decisions. Made up of knights and ranks mentioned above.

Knight

The backbone of the clan.

The Round Table

The Round Table is where most clan business is discussed and where applicants are discussed. Alle members have an equal voice in these discussions

Squire

The rank given to new members in the clan. They have to prove their worth to become a knight of the round table

WAY OF RTK

The tenets of RTK: Honour, Integrity, Loyalty, Respect and Brotherhood.

Honour

As RTK, we have a deep sense of personal integrity coupled with respect for others.

Integrity

As RTK, we demonstrate uncompromising conduct in all of our personal actions. We are honest and truthful in dealings with each other, and with those outside the clan.

Loyalty

As RTK, we have faith in and place trust in ourselves, the clan, its Tenets and in each other.

Respect

As RTK, we hold others in high regard, treating them with fairness, dignity and compassion. We respect ourselves, we respect our clanmates whether they are peers, superiors or subordinates. And every member of the Total War community

Brotherhood

As RTK, we are friends and comrades who share fellowship in a common bond. The values expressed here and in the "Tenets of the Round Table Knights".

RECRUITMENT

The knights of the round table.

What we're looking for:

  • You have a passion for Total War games
  • You are over the age of 18
  • You can read and speak English (you do not have to be fluent)
  • You have a microphone and are willing to use Discord
  • You agree to follow the RTK Code

If you apply and we accept, you will:

  • Be part of a prestigious Total War clan with a rich history of being on the forefront of competitive multiplayer Total War
  • Interact and play with a group of veteran Total War players that understand the game well and want to play it to their best ability
  • Have access to our Discord and our forums
  • Have full access to our stock of fine ales and wines served in our castle
  • Be able to berate and flog the peasants and court jesters at your whim
  • Swear fealty to our noble King Mercurius

Please post your applications on our Discord. Go and play and talk with us in the meanwhile. This greatly increases the chances of a successful application.
