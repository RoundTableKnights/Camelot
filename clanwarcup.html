All clans of Total War: Warhammer II are invited to compete in a clan war tournament to decide who will be the Clan War Champions!

The Clan War Cup will feature two stages:

Group stage:

Each group contains three or four clans. Each clan will play a clan war against the other clans in its group.

The team with the most wins goes through. In the event of a tie, the clan with the most points from its clan wars goes through.

If a team drops out, results against it are disregarded.

Knockout stage:

The four teams that won their group proceed to a single elimination knock-out stage (semi-final and final) . The semi-final and final each consist of a clan war.

Schedule:

Clans are free to arrange their clan wars within the schedule below according to availability.

First group stage game: By Sunday 12 September

Second group stage game: By Sunday 26 September

Third group stage game: By Sunday 10 October

Semi-finals: By Sunday 31 October

Grand final: Saturday 14 November (unless agreed otherwise)

Casting/Replays

Replays should be saved in the RTK "clan war replays" discord channel.

When you have arranged your clan war, please post the time in the "clan war cup" discord channel.

Clan War Format:

Each clan war consists of:

  • SIX 1v1s - 1 point each
  • THREE 2v2s - 2 points each
  • ONE 3v3 - 3 points

Blind picks - Each faction may only be picked once. Picks are exchanged one week before the clan war in question using the example pick sheet. Each side must fill out the columns for factions and players.

A faction can only be played once per clan, and each clan must play every faction. Subfactions count as the main faction.

Players may play in multiple games, but no more than once in the same type of game (ie, at most a player could play in one 1v1, in one 2v2 and in the 3v3).

Army Limits

1v1:

Fruit Rules (Ripe) apply.

Funds: Large


2v2:

Each individual army must comply with Fruit Rules (Ripe)

Funds: 10 000 per player

In addition each team is capped at:

  • Max 3 heroes (in addition to lords)
  • Max 4 SEMs
  • Max 6 SEs
  • Max 3 summon spells and/or abilities
  • Max 2 nets, only 1 of which may be targetable
  • Max 1 mortis engine effect
  • Max 9 skirmish cav/chariots per team
  • Max 9 360° shooting units
  • Max 1 guardian ability
  • Max 2 boar/tuskgor chariots (whether single entity or multi entity)
  • No duplicate named heroes (Gotrek/Felix)
  • No duplicate spells

3v3:

Each individual army must comply with Fruit Rules (Ripe)

Funds: 10 000 per player

In addition each team is capped at:

  • Max 4 heroes (in addition to lords)
  • Max 5 SEM
  • Max 8 SE
  • Max 3 summon spells and/or abilities
  • Max 2 nets, only 1 of which may be targetable
  • Max 1 mortis engine effect
  • Max 12 skirmish cav/chariots per team
  • Max 12 360° shooting units
  • Max 1 guardian ability
  • Max 2 boar/tuskgor chariots (whether single entity or multi entity)
  • No duplicate named heroes (Gotrek/Felix).
  • No duplicate spells

Rules of Play

Fruit Rules (Ripe) apply.

In team games, rules of play apply to the army as a whole as if the team were a single army (ie, only one player must attack for the team to count as attacking).
